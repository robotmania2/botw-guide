From anywhere, warp to the Shrine of Resurrection. Then, walk or shield surf to the Temple of Time. Next, pray to the statue to receive a heart or some stamina. Finally, get to the bell tower and talk to the Old Man to receive the paraglider. He will reveal that he is King Rhoam Bosphoramus Hyrule and give you a new Main Quest called Seek Out Impa.
